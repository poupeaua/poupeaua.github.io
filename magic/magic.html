About

I fell in love with card tricks when I was 17 years old. I started by practicing hours, hours and hours without counting doing magic card tricks in front of a mirror.

During my engineer school I had the chance to be member of the Club de Magie de Grenoble INP. It was a great opportunity to perform card tricks during galas, student parties and ceremonies. I continue to teach card magic in this club now.
